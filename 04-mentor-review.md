## What it's for

This is where the high-level thinking happens — code quality, architectural decisions, patterns you can't yet see, assumptions you haven't questioned. It's not a tutorial and it's not a debugging session. If you show up with questions your peers could have answered, you've wasted both your time and the mentor's.
